Participating in Our Studies

Be a part of our research: we have several opportunities to participate in ongoing studies!

For Schools

Your school can partner with the Language Learning Lab to help us understand how kids learn language through fun and engaging studies! Find out more here!

For Adult Participants

Games with Words

We have several ongoing experiments in our Web-based laboratory, where you can participate in language learning studies from your own personal computer!

Boston College Undergraduates

Boston College undergraduate students are eligible to participate in our adult, in-lab studies on language learning and can sign up on Sona Systems or email us for more information.
