Contact Me

For inquires about joining the lab, see here.

Bugs in our online experiments should be reported to info@gameswithwords.org.

Follow-ups to applications for posted jobs can be sent to languagelearninglab@bc.edu.

Inquiries from reporters and from current and former students, advisees, and collaborators should be sent to me directly and I will reply as soon as possible.

All other inquiries can be sent to joshua.hartshorne@bc.edu. Unfortunately, sheer volume prevents me from replying to every inquiry. I prioritize inquires that support the core mission of the laboratory. If I do not get back to you, I apologize and I hope you will forgive me.

While I love speaking with young science enthusiasts, I am not usually able to respond to inquiries from students who wish to interview me for a class project. This is so I can ensure I have enough time for outreach activities with broader impact, such as speaking with reporters, participating in Skype a Scientist, writing articles for the general press and for the lab blog, and so on. If you think you have a good argument for why responding to you is a better use of my community outreach time than those other activities, please make your case and I'll respond if I can.
